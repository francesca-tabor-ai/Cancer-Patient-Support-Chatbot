# Rigshospitalet Cancer Patient Chatbot - User Guide

## Welcome

Welcome to the Rigshospitalet Cancer Patient Support Chatbot. This guide will help you understand how to use the chatbot effectively and safely.

## What is This Chatbot?

The Rigshospitalet Cancer Patient Chatbot is an AI-powered assistant designed to provide information and support to cancer patients at Rigshospitalet, Copenhagen's largest public teaching hospital. The chatbot can help answer questions about cancer care, treatment options, side effects, and hospital services.

### Important Notice

**This chatbot is NOT a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.** Always consult your healthcare provider for specific medical concerns. The information provided is for educational purposes only.

## Getting Started

### 1. Sign In

To use the chatbot, you must first sign in with your Manus account. Click the "Sign In" button on the homepage.

### 2. Grant Consent

When you first access the chatbot, you will be asked to provide consent for data processing. This is required under GDPR and EU AI Act regulations. Your data will be:

- Stored securely and encrypted
- Used only to provide chatbot services
- Retained according to hospital policies
- Protected under GDPR and EU AI Act

You can withdraw your consent at any time by contacting the hospital.

### 3. Choose Your Language

The chatbot supports both English and Danish. You can switch between languages at any time using the language selector in the header.

## Using the Chatbot

### Starting a Conversation

1. Type your question in the message box at the bottom of the screen
2. Press Enter or click the Send button
3. Wait for the chatbot to respond (usually within a few seconds)

### Example Questions

You can ask the chatbot about:

- **Treatment Information**: "What is chemotherapy?" "What are the side effects of radiation therapy?"
- **Hospital Services**: "Where is the oncology department?" "How do I schedule an appointment?"
- **Support Resources**: "Are there support groups for cancer patients?" "What mental health services are available?"
- **Recovery and Care**: "How can I manage fatigue during treatment?" "What foods should I eat during chemotherapy?"

### Understanding Responses

The chatbot will provide information based on current medical knowledge and hospital resources. Each response includes:

- An AI disclosure notice (in your first interaction)
- A medical disclaimer when discussing medical topics
- Clear, accessible language
- Encouragement to discuss concerns with your healthcare team

## Safety Features

### AI Disclosure

You will see a warning banner at the top of the chat interface reminding you that you are communicating with an AI system. This is required under the EU AI Act for transparency.

### Medical Disclaimer

When the chatbot discusses medical topics, it will include a disclaimer reminding you to seek professional medical advice for your specific situation.

### Human Oversight

If you need to speak with a human medical professional, you can request a review at any time:

1. Click the "Request Human Review" button in the header
2. Describe why you need human assistance
3. Submit your request
4. A medical staff member will review your request and respond

## Privacy and Data Protection

### What Data is Collected?

The chatbot collects:

- Your messages and questions
- The chatbot's responses
- Timestamps of interactions
- Your consent records
- Technical information (IP address, browser type)

### How is Data Protected?

- All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- Access is restricted to authorized personnel only
- Data is stored in compliance with GDPR
- Audit logs track all system access

### Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Withdraw consent at any time
- Request a copy of your data

To exercise these rights, contact the hospital's Data Protection Officer.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can the chatbot diagnose my condition?

No. The chatbot cannot and will not provide medical diagnoses. It can provide general information about conditions and treatments, but you must consult your healthcare provider for diagnosis.

### Can I trust the information provided?

The chatbot is trained on evidence-based medical information and hospital resources. However, it is an AI system and may occasionally provide incomplete or incorrect information. Always verify important information with your healthcare team.

### What if the chatbot doesn't understand my question?

Try rephrasing your question in simpler terms. If the chatbot still cannot help, use the "Request Human Review" feature to speak with a medical professional.

### Can I use the chatbot in an emergency?

**No.** This chatbot is not for emergencies. In case of a medical emergency, call 112 (Denmark emergency number) or go to the nearest emergency department immediately.

### How long are my conversations stored?

Conversations are stored according to hospital data retention policies. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting the Data Protection Officer.
